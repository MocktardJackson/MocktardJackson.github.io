    Ollivier Sanscolline #

    Description

    Oliver Hillless is a 35 year old male human innkeeper. He has extremely long, braided, auburn hair and brown eyes. He has rough chocolate skin. He stands 152cm (4'11”) tall and has a regular build. He has an oblong, mediocre face with a long, braided chinstrap beard.

    He smells heavily of tobacco.

    Personality Traits

    He quietly worships Lathander, God of spring, dawn, birth, youth, vitality, athletics. (Neutral Good) He shows very little empathy towards others. He is non-materialistic. He only dresses in very expensive clothes. He collects sea shells.
